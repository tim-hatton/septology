Hot Dogs

Here is my unique hot dog recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Chopped Onions

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to 350°.
  2. Place dogs on grill.
  3. Occasionally roll dogs for even cooking.
  4. Place cooked dogs in buns.
  5. Apply mustard, relish, and onions to taste.
  6. Eat and enjoy!
